10 MW Solar Power Generation – Our Commitment to Green Energy

At Kamal Oils & Foods, sustainability is at the core of our operations. As part of our long-term environmental strategy, we have invested in a 10 MW Solar Power Generation System to significantly reduce our carbon footprint and dependence on non-renewable energy sources.

Key Highlights

  • ✔ 10 MW solar power capacity installed.
  • ✔ Supports our manufacturing units with clean, renewable energy.
  • ✔ Reduces CO₂ emissions by thousands of tonnes annually.
  • ✔ Enables long-term cost efficiency and energy independence.
  • ✔ Aligns with global sustainability standards and green practices.

Our Impact & Vision

Our Vision

A Sustainable Tomorrow, Built Today

We aim to expand our renewable energy capacity further and continue adopting innovative, eco-friendly technologies. Our goal is to make our operations greener, more efficient, and aligned with India's national sustainable development goals.

  • Expand renewable energy capacity across all operational facilities.
  • Integrate advanced eco-friendly technologies to reduce environmental impact.
  • Improve energy efficiency and resource optimisation in manufacturing processes.
  • Align business practices with India's national sustainable development and climate goals.
Our Impact

Real Numbers. Real Change.

Through this solar initiative, we are able to meet a substantial percentage of our electricity requirements through clean, renewable solar energy, contributing to:

  • Reduced greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Lower energy consumption from fossil-fuel based sources.
  • A cleaner and more sustainable operational footprint.
  • Improved environmental responsibility across our value chain.
